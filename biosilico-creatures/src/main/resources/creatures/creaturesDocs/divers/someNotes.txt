	(vivant) → règne → embranchement → classe → ordre → famille → genre → espèce
	RECOFGE est le sigle mnémotechnique des 7 rangs principaux: Règne/Embranchement/Classe/Ordre/Famille/Genre/Espèce.
Kingdom, phylum/division, class, order, family, genus, species. "Domain", a level above kingdom, has become popular in recent years, but has not (yet) been accepted into the codes
